Context Analysis - Ghana

The map below provides context data on mangroves forests in eastern Ghana. Refer to the layers list (top right) to toggle datasets.

The map displays mangrove forest populations in 2016, as well as deforestation areas between 1996-2016. Two project areas - Songor and Fiaxor - are illustrated, as well as major settlement zones. 

Satellite data provides no reference zone for the recent presence of mangrove forests in the Songor / East Ada areas. In the Keta areas, we likewise see no recent cover. 

It is likely that mangrove cover loss occurred decades before satellite images are available, as local accounts suggest that they did once exist here in the 20th century.

Governmental data resources are lacking to support this. However, academic studies can be conducted and drawn upon to determine their presence and we intend to pursue this option to determine our baselines for reforestation.
